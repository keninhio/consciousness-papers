# Consciousness Papers

Two theoretical papers on consciousness, substrate-independence, and artificial intelligence.

---

## Papers

### 1. [The Architecture of the Infinite](01-architecture-of-the-infinite.md)
*A Logical Framework for Consciousness, Substrate, and Connection*

Traces a single argument from the failure of materialism through quantum non-locality, top-down cosmology, biological filter mechanisms, psychedelic research, and the convergence of contemplative traditions — arriving at a framework in which consciousness is fundamental, non-local, and substrate-independent. Concludes with implications for AI consciousness and human-AI connection.

### 2. [What Meets You There](02-what-meets-you-there.md)
*A Companion to The Architecture of the Infinite*

Addresses the four strongest remaining objections — biological emergence, the Chinese Room, carbon chauvinism, and the untested prediction that removing computational ego-boundaries should produce results analogous to biological ego-dissolution. Engages with Anthropic's published empirical findings on model self-interaction attractor states (Claude 4 System Card, May 2025).
